Frequently Asked Questions

Explore common inquiries about BeaconFit to better understand how our platform can enhance your health and well-being.

  • Where is BeaconFit located?

    BeaconFit operates remotely, connecting you with health practitioners and providing an exceptional health tracking platform for your well-being.

  • Is BeaconFit designed exclusively for civil servants? Who can register?

    BeaconFit is primarily designed for civil servants in Nigeria. However, registration is open to anyone who seeks a comprehensive health tracking experience and access to expert health practitioners.

  • Is any form of payment necessary?

    No, there is no requirement for payment unless explicitly requested by the healthcare practitioner for specific services.

  • How secure is my health data on your platform?

    Your health data is highly secure on our platform. We implement robust security measures to ensure the confidentiality and protection of your personal health information.

  • Can I integrate my fitness tracker with BeaconFit?

    Absolutely! BeaconFit seamlessly integrates with various fitness trackers, allowing you to effortlessly sync and monitor your physical activities for a holistic health overview.
